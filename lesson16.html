Since 1921, Dasaprakash™ has been serving millions of happy customers with Authentic South Indian Vegetarian Cuisine. Dasaprakash™ provides various hospitality experiences like fine-dine, quick service restaurants and boarding & lodgings. Well-known hotelier and the Director of Dasaprakash Group, Late K. Balakrishna Rao (1935-2013), the eldest son of K. Seetharama Rao and Kamalabai, was a stalwart and a forerunner in the hospitality industry.

Place Description

Dasaprakash is the place to enjoy authentic South Indian, North Indian, Oriental and Continental delicacies in Delhi. This casual dining restaurant offers great food at extremely affordable prices. The restaurant offers legendary hospitality along with offering an array of dishes.
